Featured projects

Gift Time

ReactJSTypescriptMaterial UISupabaseReact QueryNetlify

I always enjoy making apps for work or myself but one of the most satisfying things to do is make something that your friends and family can use. During the 2020 holiday season I made an app called Group Gift to help my family organize wish lists between everyone. This app is the latest iteration of that app which I created in preparation for the 2021 holiday season. Users can create wish lists for themselves by adding gifts that they would like. Every group they join will then provide those group members with access to each user's wish list. Everyone in the group can claim gifts and coordinate to knock out each item in everyone's list. It is a React app that employs Material UI for styling, Supabase combined with React Query for auth, persistence and querying optimization, and React Hook Form for easier forms. I used Netlify for easy CI/CD.
